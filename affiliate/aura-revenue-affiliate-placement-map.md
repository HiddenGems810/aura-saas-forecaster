## Compliance Risks Avoided

- No Amazon product images copied or stored locally.
- No Amazon star ratings or review counts displayed on public pages.
- No hardcoded prices displayed on public pages.
- No copied Amazon product descriptions or reviews.
- Affiliate links use `rel="sponsored nofollow noopener noreferrer"`.
- Affiliate CTAs are text-only and clearly leave the site.
- No claims that products were personally tested.
- No recommendations for medical, supplement, weapons, alcohol, adult, gambling, crypto, or brand-unsafe products.
- No excessive link density above the fold.
- No ads or affiliate links placed inside calculator controls.
- Pages include original buying guidance and internal links so they are not thin product grids.

## Manual Pre-Go-Live Checks

- Use Amazon Associates SiteStripe or approved link tools to replace exact-search tagged destinations with product-specific Special Links where desired.
- Confirm every selected product is currently available and appropriate.
- Verify rating, review count, Prime availability, and product page quality internally before promoting any recommendation in campaigns.
- Do not add public ratings, review counts, or price claims unless they are served through Amazon-approved APIs and kept compliant.
